# Iguana Classic Release Plan

This repository outlines the roadmap and objectives for the upcoming release of Iguana Classic, aimed at supporting customers using both Iguana 5 and Iguana 6 products. The key goal is to deliver an updated version that is 100% backwards compatible with our most widely adopted and trusted products, ensuring a smooth, disruption-free upgrade for all users.

Iguana 6.1.5 has established itself as our most popular production release, earning praise for its unmatched stability and overall ease of use. This reliability is evidenced by company-wide support data: on a daily basis, there are virtually no technical support issues reported for Iguana 6. Most customer interactions currently relate to business operations—such as price negotiations and compliance questionnaires—rather than technical problems. For many users, Iguana 6.1.5 is considered the “perfect product.”

However, despite its stability and user satisfaction, it is essential that we update Iguana 6.1.5. The primary motivation for this upgrade centers on security enhancements. As the regulatory landscape evolves—especially for customers involved in the handling of sensitive Patient Health Information (PHI)—keeping software compliant with the latest security standards is absolutely critical. Emerging threats, updated compliance requirements (such as HIPAA and GDPR), and best practices in application security necessitate that we modernize the product without compromising the features and dependability our customers rely on.

For these reasons, Iguana 6.3 Classic will be a mandatory upgrade for all enterprise customers, with particular emphasis on those handling significant volumes of PHI. This update ensures continued protection of sensitive information, reduces organizational risk, and positions our customers to meet current and future compliance obligations with confidence.

In summary, while Iguana 6.1.5 remains unrivaled for its robust performance, the 6.3 Classic release is a proactive step to maintain our commitment to security, compliance, and customer trust, safeguarding both our users and the patients whose data they manage.
