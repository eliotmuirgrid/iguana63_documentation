# Decentralized [Affiliate Model](../affiliates.md)

**General Theme:**  
iNTERFACEWARE is moving towards a decentralized business model that puts customer choice at the center. Instead of a single, uniform approach, customers now have access to a **range of affiliate partners**—each with unique specialties, local expertise, and value propositions. This empowers customers to find the *best fit* for their specific business needs, industry requirements, and regional context.

## How Choice Works in This Context

- **Range of Affiliates:** Customers are presented with multiple affiliates, each offering different specialties, strengths, or value propositions.
- **Comparison:** Customers can evaluate affiliates based on price, quality, experience, localization, and ability to customize.
- **Personalized Fit:** This flexibility makes it easier for each customer to find the affiliate(s) best aligned with their requirements for customization, flexibility, or support.
- **Empowerment:** By choosing affiliates who understand their unique setting, customers enjoy better alignment and a more personalized service experience.

---

## Example: The Value of Local Market Understanding

Imagine a healthcare organization based in Ontario is looking for integration solutions to connect their Electronic Medical Records (EMR) system with external health data platforms. With the **decentralized affiliate model**, iNTERFACEWARE presents several affiliate partners to choose from—some local to Ontario and others based in different provinces or even internationally.

The Ontario-based customer may **prefer to work with an Ontario-based affiliate** for several reasons:

- **Local Market Insight:** An Ontario affiliate is likely to be familiar with provincial healthcare regulations, privacy requirements (such as PHIPA), and eHealth Ontario standards. This expertise helps ensure compliance and reduces onboarding time.
- **Contextual Understanding:** The Ontario affiliate understands the specific challenges faced by local hospitals and clinics, such as unique referral workflows, language preferences (including French-speaking populations), and regional funding models.
- **Relevant Experience:** Affiliates rooted in Ontario may already have a portfolio of local clients facing similar integration issues, making them better equipped with proven solutions and local references.

By being able to **compare affiliates not just on price and services, but also geographic and contextual relevance**, the customer is empowered to **select a partner who truly understands their local market and operational landscape**.

This leads to:
- _Faster project implementation_
- _Fewer misunderstandings_
- _Solutions optimized for local needs_

**In summary (Local Theme):**  
For an Ontario-based customer, the ability to select an affiliate who shares their regional expertise ensures challenges are addressed in the right context, leading to more successful, compliant, and user-friendly solutions. The decentralized affiliate model makes this kind of tailored partnership possible, emphasizing the power of customer choice.
