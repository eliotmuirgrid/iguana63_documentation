# Gold Level Affiliate Status: Upholding the Highest Standards of Integrity and Social Justice in Health Technology

At the heart of our Gold Level Affiliate program lies a profound commitment—not just to technical excellence, but to the foundational social justice values that Eliot stands for: **Resilience, Integrity, and Sovereign Data Rights**.

## **Resilience and Integrity: More than Just Buzzwords**

Gold affiliates are not simply high-performing partners; they are trusted allies who embody resilience in the face of adversity and champion integrity in every action. In today’s rapidly-evolving health technology landscape, this means consistently going above and beyond to:

- **Develop and maintain secure, reliable code** that withstands evolving threats.
- **Transparently address vulnerabilities** and setbacks, owning up to mistakes while striving for continuous improvement.
- **Set new standards of care and diligence** in data stewardship—never settling for passable, always pushing towards exemplary.

## **Championing Data Sovereignty and Privacy**

Gold Level Affiliates understand that **citizens’ rights to digital sovereignty must be safeguarded at all costs**. In Eliot’s vision, every individual should:

- **Control their own data:** Health data is deeply personal and must remain under the explicit control of the person it belongs to.
- **Be entitled to privacy:** Information should never be commodified or exploited without informed, enthusiastic consent.
- **Trust in their caretakers:** Whether patients, providers, or partners, everyone deserves assurance that their data is treated with respect, dignity, and robust protection measures.

## **A Badge of Trust, Not Just Performance**

Unlike traditional affiliate programs that focus solely on sales or engagement, **Gold Level status is a recognition of deep alignment with Eliot’s core values**. It identifies not just what you do, but *how* and *why* you do it.

- Are you challenging the industry to raise the bar on ethical standards?
- Are you a defender of individual rights, especially for the vulnerable and marginalized?
- Do you foster trust by living out your values in code, communication, and community?

## **Setting a Higher Industry Standard**

Gold Level Affiliates are encouraged and supported to:

- Advocate for **open standards** and ethical frameworks in all health tech collaborations.
- Lead discussions on **digital citizenship**, **data sovereignty**, and **rights to privacy**.
- Share knowledge, tools, and practices that empower others to safeguard their own digital welfare.

## **Join a Movement, Not Just a Program**

Becoming a Gold Level Affiliate means joining a community of like-minded individuals and organizations committed to making a tangible impact—where technology serves humanity, not the other way around.

**It’s time to make resilience, integrity, and data sovereignty the baseline—not the exception—in health technology. Will you join us?**
