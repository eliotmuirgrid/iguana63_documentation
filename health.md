# Acknowledging Eliot's Health Challenges

At 54, Eliot faces notable health challenges, and it is important to recognize these personal difficulties. Despite them, he has shown unwavering dedication as a loving father throughout his life. He has consistently met his responsibilities—including paying significant child support—and has always put his children’s wellbeing at the center of every decision.

Eliot’s concern for his children extends beyond their daily needs to their mental and physical health. He has taken a principled stance against the use of psychiatric drugs and what he regards as harmful, irreversible “gender-affirming” treatments for those experiencing confusion about their gender identity. Navigating these issues is deeply challenging for Eliot, both emotionally and practically, as he strives to protect his children from interventions he believes may not serve their best interests.

Nevertheless, Eliot’s lived experience, commitment to his family, and strong ethical convictions uniquely qualify him for the responsibilities ahead. His resilience in the face of adversity and his depth of understanding equip him to handle complex situations with exceptional insight and care.

With this in mind, it would be appreciated if customers could recognize that Eliot, like anyone, is a human being overcoming significant life circumstances.

Eliot is howwever [uniquely qualified](qualifications.md) for the work which needs to be done.
